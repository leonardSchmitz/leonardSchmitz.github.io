Leonard Schmitz

I am a member of the Algebraische und Geometrische Methoden in der Datenanalyse group, lead by Carlos Amendola, at the TU Berlin. Before that, I was part of Bernd Sturmfels' Nonlinear Algebra group at the MPI MiS Leipzig.

Just recently I received my PhD at the University of Greifswald under the supervision of Joscha Diehl. My thesis received the University’s PhD Prize. I hold a Master’s degree from RWTH Aachen.

Email: lschmitz (at) math . tu-berlin . de

My work is supported by the CRC TRR 388 Rough Analysis, Stochastic Dynamics and Related Fields.
